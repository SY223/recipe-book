This refreshing salad combines the crispness of cucumber, the juiciness of tomatoes, and the zing of onion, all brought together with a simple olive oil and red cider vinegar dressing. Perfect for a light lunch or as a side dish.

15 Minutes Vegetable Salad

Preparation Time: 10 minutes

Cooking Time: None

Servings: 4

Difficulty Level Beginner

Ingredients

  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2 ripe tomatoes, diced
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon red cider vinegar
  • Sprinkle of salt to enhance taste

Instructions

  1. Combine onion, tomato, and cucumber in a large mixing bowl.
  2. Drizzle olive oil and red cider vinegar over the vegetables.
  3. Sprinkle ground black pepper and salt to taste.
  4. Toss everything together gently until well mixed.
  5. Serve immediately or refrigerate for 15 minutes for a chilled salad.

Tips

For extra flavor, you can add fresh herbs such as parsley or basil. If you prefer a milder onion flavor, soak the chopped onion in cold water for 5 minutes before adding to the salad.
